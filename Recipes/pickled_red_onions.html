Pickled Red Onions

Description

TPickled red onions are a staple served with Texas BBQ. Most trays of Texas BBQ are served with pickled red onions, pickles & sliced white bread. This recipe is a quick pickle. These pickled red onions go well on so many dishes in addition to BBQ such as as tacos, burgers, sandwiches & salads.

Ingredients

  • 2 large red onions
  • 1 Cup, cider vinegar
  • 1 Cup of water
  • 1 Cup of sugar
  • 2 tablespoons kosher salt
  • 1 jalapeño sliced (optional for spice)

Steps

  1. Slice onions and the optional jalapeños thin. A mandolin makes this easy work!
  2. Add the water, cider vinegar, sugar and salt to a medium pot and bring to a boil. Whisk until the sugar is dissolved.
  3. Place the sliced red onions (mixed with jalapeño if you like) in mason jars or a food safe container.
  4. Pour the hot liquid over the onions. Allow the onions to soften and cool. Then screw on the lid and store in the refrigerator.
  5. Pickled onions will last up to 3 weeks in an airtight mason jar in the refrigerator.
